About This Project and the Developers

The purpose of this site is to provide the most accurate prediction of the liklihood of an outbreak in your age-group based of novel COVID-19 for an individual. We aim to accomplish this buy compiling the most up to date and accurate information available to the public. This is done by combing characterisits of the user and artificial intelligence regression models to preduct the liklehood of contraction. The datasets inluded in this project are:

  • Corona Virus Dataset
  • Census Data for Population Demographics of Counties
  • Google Moiblity Data
